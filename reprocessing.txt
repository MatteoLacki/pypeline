To reprocess raw folders:
1. select the folders you want to reprocess in explorer or a containing folder (then all folders inside will get analysed).
2. right click , click send to, click on _PLGS.
3. follow the instructions

The terminal will ask you for fastas.
Write in a single value from the following possibilities:
human, ecoli, hye, leishmania, mouse, wheat, yeast
OR
provide a valid absolute windows path
(you can drag'n'drop a file from explorer).

To reprocess symphony results
1. select the folders (with the xml files from symphony, not the raw folders) you want to reprocess in explorer or a containing folder (then all folders inside will get analysed).
2. right click , click send to, click on _REPROCESS.
3. follow the instructions
